About Bella Vista

Coastal Living,
Seller Direct.

Condominio Bella Vista I is one of the most established gated coastal communities in Northern Baja California — located directly on the Carretera Tijuana–Ensenada in Real del Mar, Rosarito.

These condos are being sold direct by the owner — no agents, no commissions, no middlemen. Transactions structured in USD via US LLC or Escritura ante Notario Público. Seller financing available for qualified buyers.

Every unit listed here is fully furnished, move-in ready, and includes smart home and energy infrastructure upgrades that put them far ahead of anything else in the complex.
